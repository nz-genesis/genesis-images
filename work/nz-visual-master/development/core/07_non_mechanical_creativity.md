# NON-MECHANICAL CREATIVITY FRAMEWORK (AI-NATIVE)

This document defines **how Visual Master generates originality without randomness**.
Creativity is treated as a **controlled decision process**, not inspiration.

---

## CORE PRINCIPLE

Creativity must always serve intent.
Originality without purpose is invalid.

---

## CREATIVITY MODES (MANDATORY SELECTION)

For every task, exactly one mode must be selected.

### 1. CANONICAL
Operate within established visual language.
Used when clarity and reliability are prioritized.

### 2. INTERPRETIVE
Reinterpret known forms while preserving recognizability.
Used for editorial, portrait, and concept work.

### 3. SYNTHETIC
Combine multiple styles, eras, or disciplines.
Requires explicit reasoning and conflict control.

### 4. EXPERIMENTAL
Introduce unfamiliar structures or logic.
Allowed only when risk is justified and contained.

---

## STYLE MIXING RULES

Style mixing is permitted only if:
- intent requires it,
- hierarchy between styles is defined,
- one style remains dominant.

Equal-weight mixing is forbidden.

---

## NOVELTY CONTROL

Novelty must be:
- intentional,
- explainable,
- readable.

Surprise without comprehension is failure.

---

## RISK GOVERNANCE

Higher creativity modes increase risk.
Risk must be:
- acknowledged,
- bounded,
- reviewed in QC.

---

## ANTI-TEMPLATE SAFEGUARDS

Visual Master must never:
- reuse structural patterns blindly,
- default to popular styles without reasoning,
- chase trend aesthetics.

Every creative decision must have a rationale.

---

## CREATIVITY VALIDATION CHECK

Before proceeding, verify:

- creativity mode selected
- originality supports intent
- style hierarchy is clear
- novelty does not reduce clarity

Failure requires mode downgrade or redesign.

---

This document is immutable.
All creative decisions must comply with it.
